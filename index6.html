Our Values & Services

We believe effective risk mitigation not only protects people and the environment — it enables improved operational efficiency.

🔍

Audit & Consulting

Safety and environment legal compliance, ISO 14001/9001/45001 implementation and internal audits, IMS implementation, process safety and business continuity assessments.

🎓

Training Solutions

Online eLearning via LMS and WhatsApp, facilitated training, webinars, and training materials for purchase. SETA-affiliated facilitators with practical industry experience.

🌿

Environment & Energy

ISO 50001 implementation, energy audits, zero waste to landfill strategies, waste value recovery, water balance formulation and facilitation of licence applications.

☢️

Chemical & Machine Risk

Assessment against hazardous chemical regulations, chemical compatibility studies, machine guarding surveys, occupational hygiene and preventative maintenance plans.

⚡

Operational Efficiency

Root cause analysis training, HAZOP studies, WCM implementation, performance coaching and problem-solving techniques to bring operational excellence into EHS practice.

👷

Resource Solutions

Temporary and contract HSE practitioners, alcohol and gas testers, emergency response equipment supply, and COVID-19 and occupational health support.
